## **Project Overview**

"Botani Quest" is an educational, web-based (PC) pixel-art platformer designed to facilitate foundational biology learning (specifically plant anatomy) for 4th-grade students. The core challenge of this project was transforming scientific concepts—typically consumed passively from textbooks—into an engaging, interactive exploration. Through a game-based learning approach, this project successfully merges instructional objectives with fun and challenging gameplay mechanics.

## **1. Strategic Objectives**

The main goal was to increase student motivation and memory retention regarding plant biology through active participation. Key strategic objectives included:

- **Drive Active Learning:** Shifting the student's role from a passive listener to an active explorer who must interact with the learning environment to solve problems.
- **Enhance Retention Through Play:** Leveraging in-game reward systems and level progression so that the functions and parts of plants are memorized naturally.
- **Create a Stress-Free Learning Environment:** Reducing academic anxiety by providing a "safe space" where children can make mistakes (such as failing a jump or answering incorrectly) and try again without fear of penalty.

### **2. Instructional Design & Game Mechanics**

To ensure the game was not only entertaining but also highly educational, I integrated several instructional design principles into the game's structure:

- **Core Gameplay Integration:** Educational content (e.g., understanding the functions of roots, stems, and leaves) is not merely tacked on; it is woven directly into the core gameplay loop. Players must interact with these concepts to advance to the next level.
- **Immediate Feedback:** Providing instant visual and auditory responses whenever a player collects an educational item, a psychological tactic proven to reinforce cognitive memory in young learners.
- **Pixel-Art Visual Approach:** Utilizing a 2D pixel-art aesthetic that is highly visually appealing and familiar to children, while also ensuring the game remains lightweight and runs smoothly via web browsers.

## **Summary of Gameplay Structure**

The learning journey is designed to feel like an integral part of the player's adventure:

- **Environmental Exploration (Platforming):** Players navigate their character through various obstacles visually themed around nature, ecosystems, and botany.
- **Knowledge Collection:** Players gather specific objects throughout the levels that unlock bite-sized facts about plant functions.
- **Integrated Cognitive Challenges:** Features mechanics where players must apply their foundational biology knowledge to overcome specific hurdles or complete a learning stage.
