# Agent Network

### Video Highlight

## Project Overview

The AmarthaLink Agent Network project aims to empower partners with the knowledge and tools needed to manage a network of agents effectively. The core of this initiative is a series of structured learning videos that translate complex financial processes into accessible, engaging, and actionable training content.

### **1. Strategic Objectives**

The primary goal was to ensure that every Agent Partner could:

- **Understand the Ecosystem:** Grasp the landscape of AmarthaFin as a digital financial service platform.
- **Master Product Knowledge:** Understand the functions, criteria, and registration processes for AmarthaLink agents.
- **Execute Transactions:** Gain proficiency in "Poket" (digital wallet) for services like Top-Ups, Cash-Outs, and Money Transfers.
- **Drive Acquisition:** Learn the correct schemes for acquiring both organic and non-organic agents.

### **2. Content Strategy & Methodology**

To maximize learning retention, a multi-format instructional design was adopted. Each topic was matched with the most effective delivery method:

- **Video Lectures:** Used for conceptual and foundational knowledge, such as "Who can become an Agent?" and "Selling Points of AmarthaFin."
- **Video Simulations:** Used for practical, scenario-based learning. For example, a simulation featuring "Ibu Mitra" was developed to demonstrate how to serve a customer’s Top-Up request in a real-world setting.
- **Explainer Videos & Motion Graphics:** Reserved for procedural tasks like "Steps to Register" or "How to Transfer Money," where visual cues and UI walkthroughs are critical.
- **Interactive Learning Media:** Provided for hands-on, risk-free practice. This allows learners to actively navigate the AmarthaFin app interface for tasks like agent registration and Poket digital wallet top-ups.

### **3. Key Project Components**

- **Detailed Curriculum Mapping:** The project began with a robust Outline that mapped competencies to specific learning objectives. This ensured that every piece of content served a direct purpose in the agent's journey. *Example:* The topic "Transaksi Agen AmarthaLink dengan Poket" was broken down into specific simulations for Top-Ups and Cash-Outs to ensure hands-on mastery.
- **Scripting & Storyboarding:** Scripts were tailored to the persona of the audience. Lecture Scripts focused on a professional yet encouraging tone ("Hi A-Team!"). Simulation Scripts utilized relatable characters and conflict-resolution arcs (e.g., a customer forgetting a payment deadline) to make the training memorable.

## **Summary of Content Structure**

- **AmarthaFin (Video Lecture):** Recognize the digital financial landscape and agent roles.
- **AmarthaFin App Navigation (Interactive Learning Media):** Hands-on practice for agent registration and managing the Poket digital wallet (e.g., top-ups).
- **Customer Top-Up (Video Simulation):** Practical demonstration of serving customer transactions.
- **Registration Steps (Motion Graphics):** Step-by-step visual guide to agent onboarding.
