## How I track these

- **Completion rate** is calculated from LMS reporting (Moodle / SCORM-based courses). I use it as a leading indicator — if completion drops below ~50% on a module, I treat it as a design problem, not a learner problem (usually too long, wrong cognitive level, or unclear value to the learner).
- **Satisfaction** is collected via post-course Likert-scale surveys (1–4). 3.64/4 is a healthy aggregate; I aim for ≥3.5 per individual course.
- **Users Empowered** counts unique learners across all delivered courses, including FO/BP teams, head-office staff, agent partners, and the school students who used Dunia Geometri / Belajar Tulang Skuy / Botani Quest.
