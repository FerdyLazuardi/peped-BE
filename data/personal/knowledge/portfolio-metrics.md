- **Course completion rate / tingkat penyelesaian kursus / persentase course selesai / overall completion rate** → **65%**

## How I track these

- **Completion rate** is calculated from LMS reporting (Moodle / SCORM-based courses). I treat any module that drops below ~50% completion as a design problem — usually too long, wrong cognitive level, or unclear value to the learner.
- **Satisfaction** is collected via post-course Likert-scale surveys (1–4). 3.64/4 is a healthy aggregate; I aim for ≥3.5 per individual course.
- **Users Empowered** counts unique learners across all delivered courses, including FO/BP teams, head-office staff, agent partners, and the school students who used Dunia Geometri / Belajar Tulang Skuy / Botani Quest.

## Why I publish aggregate numbers

Recruiters look at portfolio sites for ~30 seconds. The four headline numbers communicate scope, sustained delivery, and learner satisfaction in one glance — without forcing them to dive into every project page.
