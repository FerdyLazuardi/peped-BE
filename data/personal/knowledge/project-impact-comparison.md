## Why N-Gain matters more than raw completion

- **Completion** = did they finish the course.
- **N-Gain** = how much they actually learned (Hake's normalized gain formula).
- A course with 100% completion and 5% N-Gain is click-through, not learning. I publish both per-project — completion shows engagement, N-Gain shows learning.
